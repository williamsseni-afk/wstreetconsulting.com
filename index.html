How It Works

From raw news to strategic advantage

01

Configure Scope

We define your monitoring domain: sectors, competitors, countries, legislation, and stakeholders that matter to your institutional mandate.

02

Daily AI Sweep

Every morning, our system scans global and local news sources, filters for strategic relevance, and rates each story's impact level: High, Medium, or Low.

03

Brief Delivery

A structured intelligence brief reaches your leadership team: executive narrative, story cards with impact ratings, and institution-specific strategic analysis.

04

Knowledge Compounds

Every brief feeds the knowledge vault. Entities, storylines, and patterns build over time. Weekly synthesis and monthly strategic reports emerge from the accumulated intelligence.
